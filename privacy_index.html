Privacy Policy

Maro & George — Effective July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Michael Beshara ("we," "us," "the sender") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with this website (MaroandGeorge.com) and related SMS text message communications sent for Maro & George's engagement ceremony and related personal events.

Information We Collect

When you RSVP through this site, we may collect your name, email address, response, and any message you choose to leave. Separately, if you provide your phone number to us directly (in person, by phone, or by other personal communication), we record it in order to send you event-related text message updates.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to manage guest RSVPs and to send you updates about this event and related personal life events (such as an associated wedding). We do not use your information for any other purpose.

SMS Consent & Communications

If you receive text messages from us, it is because you personally provided your phone number to the sender and verbally agreed to receive SMS updates about this event. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out of further messages, or HELP for assistance.

Data Sharing

Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

We will not share your opt-in to SMS messaging, or your consent status, with any third party for purposes unrelated to providing you with the services of this event communication. We may share your information with third parties who help us provide our messaging services directly (such as our messaging platform provider), solely for the purpose of delivering these text messages to you.

Data Retention & Removal

We retain your information only as long as needed to manage this event and related communications. You may request that your information be removed at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by contacting us using the information below.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information can be directed to the sender, Michael Beshara, via the contact details provided with your invitation.
